> Our solution to **Problem E: Making Room for Agriculture** in the 2025 Interdisciplinary Contest in Modeling (ICM).

## Overview

This project studies the ecological transition from a forest to an agricultural ecosystem. We develop a mathematical framework to model the interactions among crops, insects, birds, bats, chemicals, and seasonal agricultural activities.

Our analysis focuses on:

- modeling the evolution and stability of the agricultural food web;
- evaluating the effects of pesticides and herbicides;
- studying how bats and other returning species restore ecological balance;
- comparing conventional and organic farming strategies;
- balancing ecological sustainability with economic cost.

This work received the **Finalist Award (F Award)** in the 2025 ICM, placing the team in approximately the **global top 2.3%**.
